I love you

I love you so much

I could repeat it forever

Too much maybe

We met suddenly

Through poptart ravioli

And shared sheet music

I didn’t know how much

You would mean to me

You played Dnd with me

For the first time

I’m glad I could help teach you

You saw who I was

Before I told you

You’ve shared with me

So many things you love

Cartoons

Podcasts

We trade books

Write notes

Work on archery

And arsenic

I stare

When you laugh

When you smile

When you read

Because seeing you

Fills me with joy

You stay

When my brain hates me

And being myself is hard

And you don’t fully understand

Because even I don’t

I could never say

how much that means

So I love you

I love you

To Andromeda
