An old pair.


15 years

We’ve known each other

For 15 years

It’s felt so short

Yet an eternity

We’ve gone from playing Werewolf

And acting How to Train Your Dragon

To buying groceries

And filling apartments

Our weekend hangouts

Have become yearly visits

Our daily phone calls

Dwindled to none

Yet I still wonder how you’re doing

If you remember

All the things I do

We fought

And got upset

And cried

So many times

I’m almost surprised

We made it through

But of everyone who did

I’m glad it’s you
