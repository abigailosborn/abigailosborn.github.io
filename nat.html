An evil sister.


You didn’t know my name

For months after we met

Confusing me for another

Yet you somehow guessed my gender

Before I mentioned it

We play just dance

And Dnd

And do study nights

In my apartment

I’m going to miss you

When you leave

But I’ll continue

To bother you

After you move

Because

You are like my sister

Farther in distance

But never in heart
