And a new roommate.


Roommate bonding time

Has become

Any time we are in the same room

Too much maybe

It’s when we’re cooking

Even though we don’t have the ingredients

Or playing just dance

In our too small space

Sleeping on the floor

When we’re supposed to be studying

Walking faster

Than anyone can keep up with

We watch jeopardy

And queer movies

When we’re bored

We exercise because

you’re vegetarian and gluten free

Roommate withdrawal

Hits when you are gone

Work on archery

I have found a best friend

Not just on campus
