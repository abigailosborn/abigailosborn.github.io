A youngest child.


I see myself in you

I wish the world

could be kinder

I try to make it better

For people like us

But fighting

Trailblazing

Fury

Can become an inferno

That consumes your being

It burns bright

But reduces you to coal

I consider you my son

In that I will do my best

To protect you from the world

But hatred is an arrow

That can pierce most shields

I hope the world is kinder to you

Than it has been to me

I wish I could mold it for you

Instead

I must hope you are strong

Though you shouldn’t have to be
